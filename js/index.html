Interactive Web Demos from Simple Simulation Engine

Ports of C++/Python sims to JS/WebGL/Three.js. Click to launch!

  • MHD Demo (Magnetohydrodynamics Sim) - Fluid dynamics with fields.
  • GLSL Solid Modeling - Raytracing & primitives.
  • Planet Designer - Procedural planets & turbulence.
  • MolGUI Web - Molecule editor.
  • OBJ Loader - 3D model viewer.
  • Spacecraft Editor - Build & render ships.
